Air traffic in Australia

Australia is a country who has developed aviation industry. International and domestic aviation connects Australians with each other and with the rest of the world. It is also central to the Australian economy.
The Australian Government, through the department, contributes to the prosperity of the economy and the wellbeing of all Australians by fostering a viable, competitive and safe aviation industry.
This visualisation will mainly focus on domestic aviation about Australia in recent years. It will analysis the quantitative metrics about avaition and it will use graph to answer several questions about domestic aviation.

HOW DOES THE AIR TRAFFIC CHANGES
IN RECENT YEARS?

From 1984 to 2019, the total passenger, aircrafts, distance and seats grows. However, the total distance grows slower than other values, we can inference that the airlines in Australia use planes which can take more passengers.
However, these four indicators except for total distance drop sharply in 2020 due to COVID-19, they reached the number before 2000. The total distance didn't not be influenced, it may because the cargo aviation was still working and took the position of passenger aviation.

WHAT IS THE MOST POPULAR ROUTE
IN AUSTRALIA DOMESTIC LINES?

The most popular domestic line in Australia in Melbourne-Sydney, which had the largest passengers count from 2018 to 2020. The top-10 lines in Australia are also fixed in recent years, but Brisbane to Cairns and Townsville lines became one of the top-10 in 2020. The result illustrates that the passenger aviation is well developed in the big city of Australia. Gold coast is also a popular city in Australia as a tourist attraction.

WHAT IS THE DISTANCE DISTRIBUTION
ABOUNT DOMESTIC ROUTES

The distances for most of air routes are shorter than 2000km, and there are mainly between 500km and 1000km. Austrlia still has lots of short-haul routes (less than 500km). The count of short-haul routes grown from 2015 to 2019. The longest routes in Australia reaches 4000km.
In addition, longer route also means higher RPK (Revenue Passenger Kilometer) for airlines. But when distance is between 1500-2500km, the RPK doesn't become as high as we expect.

WHAT IS THE CITY CAN FLY TO MOST AIRPORTS
IN AUSTRLIA?

In Australia, the Brisbane has most domestic destinations can reach among all cities in Australia between 2015 and 2020. It may because Brisbane is one of the largest aviation hub in Australia. Following the Brisbane, the cities have most domestic routes are Adelaide and Melbourne. From the bar chart in different years, the routes for a city didn't not change a lot in recent years. What is interesing is that the Sydney airport doesn't have the most domestic routes, it mainly operates international routes.

ARRIVAL AND DEPARTURE
IN DIFFERENT STATES

From the map, we can see that Queensland and New North Wales are the most favorite destinations in Australia. In the spring and summer of Australia, the Western Australia becomes more popular from Septermeber to November. The Queensland and Victoria are popular departure state in Australia. In the winter, the departure passenger count is less than other months. Northern Territory is also the state has lowest departure passengers around the year.
